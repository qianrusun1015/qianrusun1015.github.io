Qianru Sun 孙倩茹

I am an Assistant Professor (tenure track) in the School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University, since Aug 2019. From Apr 2018 to Jul 2019, I was a Research Fellow working with Prof. Tat-Seng Chua at the National University of Singapore. From Mar 2016 to Mar 2018, I held the Lise Meitner Award Fellowship and was a Post-Doc working with Prof.Dr. Bernt Schiele and Dr. Mario Fritz at the MPI for Informatics. Since Jan 2016, I hold a Ph.D. degree from Peking University, and my PhD thesis was advised by Prof. Hong Liu. In addition, from Oct 2014 to Jan 2015, I was a visiting PhD student advised by Prof. Tatsuya Harada at the University of Tokyo.
