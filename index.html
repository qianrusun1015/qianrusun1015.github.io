Qianru Sun 孙倩茹

I am an Assistant Professor (Tenure-Track) in the School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University, since Aug 2019. Before this, I was a research fellow working with Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and leads the computer vision group in the NExT++ Lab at the National University of Singapore since Apr 2018. Before this, I held the Lise Meitner Award Fellowship and was a post-doctoral researcher working with Prof.Dr. Bernt Schiele and Dr. Mario Fritz at the Max-Planck Institute for Informatics (MPII), since Mar 2016. I hold a Ph.D. degree from Peking University since Jan 2016, and my PhD thesis was advised by Prof. Hong Liu. From Sep 2014 to Jan 2015, I was a visiting student advised by Prof. Tatsuya Harada at the University of Tokyo.

Research Interests

My research interest is to develop automatic and transferable machine learning algorithms for tackling computer vision problems.
Recently, I focus on:

  • image recognition, image generation, image feature augmentation
  • meta-learning, few-shot learning, semi-supervised learning
