Qianru Sun is currently a Research Fellow in the NExT++ lab, National University of Singapore. She is leading the computer vision group, mainly working on image recognition and data augmentation tasks. Before that, she had been a post-doctor in the department of Computer Vision and Multimodal Computing, Max-Planck Institute for Informatics in Germany for two years. She holds a PhD degree from the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University since Jan. 2016. Her research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition. Specific experiences have been made in human action recognition, anomaly event detection in videos, social relation recognition and head image inpainting in social media photos, person image generation for both low-resolution re-identification images and high-resolution fashion photos.
